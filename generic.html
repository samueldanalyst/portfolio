Sales Analysis on OLK Cookies Industries LTD.

Executive Summary: Sales Analysis on OLK Industries

Introduction:

OLK Cookies Industries LTD, a leading producer of cookies for human consumption, has embarked on a comprehensive analysis to illuminate critical aspects of its business operations. With a primary aim to provide actionable insights for refining business strategies and enhancing overall performance within the realm of cookie sales, this project focuses on conducting an in-depth sales analysis. Particular emphasis is placed on examining the impact of shipment duration on customer lifetime value.

During this analysis Cascade Grovers, Acme Grocery Store, Park & Eat LLC, Quick Bite Convenience Store, YT Restaurants was abbreviated to CG, AG, PE, QBC, YT respectively.

Objective:

The primary objective of this analysis was to identify and delineate key performance indicators (KPIs) encompassing
  • Total Transactions and year to year growth
  • Total Cookies Shipped and year to year growth
  • Total Revenue and year to year growth
  • Average Order Value
  • Conversion Rate
  • Customer Lifetime Value
  • The effect of Shipments on Customer Lifetime Value

Executed as part of an intern project, this analysis involved meticulous data cleaning and modeling procedures leveraging advanced tools such as Power Query and Power BI. By employing these sophisticated analytical tools, the project ensures a rigorous and insightful examination of OLK Industries' operational dynamics. This facilitates a nuanced understanding of various crucial facets vital for strategic decision-making and business optimization.

Data Preparation:

The raw data, spanning four years and organized into separate Excel sheets, was imported into Power Query using append and merge. The data underwent cleaning to address null values and unwanted figures. Afterward, it was loaded into Power BI for further analysis.

Key Findings:

The dashboard revealed several primary key insights for overall year:
  • Total transactions: 3172
  • Total Cookies Shipped: 1.6 million
  • Total revenue: $2.7 billion
  • Average Order Value 1,658.77
  • Conversion Rate: 519.06

Shipments Duration Segment: The categorization of shipment durations is established based on a discerning criterion: durations equal to or less than 3 are classified as expedited or fast shipments, whereas durations exceeding 3 are designated as standard or slower shipments. This meticulous classification system enables a nuanced evaluation of shipping efficiency, allowing for a more strategic and informed approach to logistics management.

Data Modeling:

A critical step in the process involved the creation of a one-to-many relationship between the fact table and dimension table. This relationship was pivotal for connecting transactional data with relevant dimensions for a comprehensive analysis.

Revenue Analysis by Year (Quarterly):

2017:

The observed pattern indicates a positive trajectory from the initial quarter through the third quarter, followed by a notable and abrupt ascent in the fourth quarter. This distinctive trend highlights the dynamic shifts in performance and a slow growth in the fourth quarter.

2018:

The area chart illustrates a consistent upward trajectory from the first quarter through the third quarter. Notably, a pronounced and rapid descent in performance is observed in the transition from the third quarter to the fourth quarter. This visual depiction underscores the need for a detailed examination of the factors influencing these trends, providing valuable insights for strategic analysis and decision-making.

2019:

An analysis of the data reveals a visible downward trend from the first quarter through the third quarter, succeeded by a noteworthy upward trajectory in the fourth quarter. This observed pattern emphasizes the dynamic nature of the data and highlights the importance of exploring the underlying factors contributing to these trends for a comprehensive understanding the rapid change during the specified period.

2020:

In the fiscal year 2020, an upward trend was noted from the first quarter to the second quarter, followed by a steep downward trend from the third quarter to the fourth quarter. This analysis of quarterly variations underscores the importance of exploring the underlying factors influencing these trends, providing valuable insights for strategic decision-making and future planning.

Customer Lifetime Value

Cascade Grocers (CG) emerges as the leading entity, boasting a figure of $2.4 billion, followed closely by Quick Bite Convenience Store (QBC) with a respectable value of $2.0 billion. This data underscores CG's prominence as the most valued customer based on Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) analysis, as depicted in the chart.

Customer Lifetime Value in respect to duration of shipment

Cascade Grocers

Cascade Grocers demonstrates a clear pattern where lower shipment durations correspond to higher Customer Lifetime Value (CLV). Specifically, CLV values of $1,927,781,557.5, $452,906,790, and $6,912,000 are associated with shipment durations of 2, 3, and 4 days, respectively.

Park & Eat LLC

Park & Eat LLC exhibits varied CLV values for shipment durations of 4 and 5 days, with corresponding values of $455,237,565 and $1,100,255,017.5, respectively.

Quick Bite Convenience Store

Quick Bite Convenience Store shows higher CLV values for shipment durations of 3 and 4 days, with respective values of $500,997,742.5 and $1,464,487,222.5.

YT Restaurant

YT Restaurant indicates elevated CLV values for shipment durations of 5 and 6 days, with values of $1,580,324,235 and $121,648,470, respectively.

Acme Grocery Store

Acme Grocery Store displays a CLV of $582,654,975 for a 2-day shipment duration.

This observation underscores a consistent relationship between shipment duration and CLV across various customers. Shorter shipment durations tend to correlate with higher CLV values, while longer durations are associated with lower CLV values. This emphasizes the significance of timely delivery and efficient logistics in maximizing customer lifetime value and overall business performance for each customers.

Recommendation:

Optimization of Shipping Processes:

Given the impact of shipment duration on customer satisfaction and business performance, it is recommended to conduct a comprehensive review of shipping processes. This may involve streamlining operations to reduce shipping durations, particularly for expedited shipments. Improved logistics efficiency can contribute to enhanced customer experience and loyalty.

Continuous Monitoring and Adaptation:

The dynamic nature of performance trends, as evident from the yearly analyses, underscores the importance of continuous monitoring and adaptability. OLK Store should establish a proactive approach to monitor market changes, customer behaviors, and internal operations, allowing for timely adjustments to business strategies.

Conclusion:

In conclusion, the sales analysis conducted on OLK Industries LTD. using Power BI has provided valuable insights into various facets of the business, ranging from customer transactions to revenue and production costs. The detailed examination of key performance indicators and trends over the years has offered a comprehensive understanding of the business dynamics. The findings emphasize the need for strategic adaptations in shipping processes and customer engagement strategies. Continuous monitoring and proactive decision-making are crucial for navigating the evolving market landscape. By implementing the recommended strategies and maintaining a flexible approach, OLK Cookies Industries can position itself for sustained growth, improved customer relations, and optimized operational efficiency. This analysis serves as a foundation for informed decision-making and strategic planning to drive the future success of OLK Cookies Industries.
